Chili con carne

Description

A delicious and hearty chili con carne that is also easy to make.The small addtion of brown
sugar and touch of vinegar in combination with the chili powder gives the soup am inticing flavor
trio. And more or less to suit your individual tastes.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons of vegitable occasionally
  • i cup of diced onions
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 1/2 pounds of lean ground beef
  • i can of whole peeled tomatoes
  • 1 can navy beans, with juice
  • 1 can chili beans in sauce
  • 1 can black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1/2 cup of diced, green bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup diced celery
  • 1/4 cup light brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons white vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon salt

Steps

Heat oil in a stockpot over medium heat. Add onions and garlic; cook and stir until
tender, about 5 minutes. Add ground beef; cook and stir until browned and crumbly,
5 to 7 minutes. Mix in tomatoes, navy beans, chili beans, black beans, green bell
pepper, celery, brown sugar, vinegar, chili powder, and salt. Bring to a boil. Reduce
heat to medium-low and let simmer until thick, at least 1 hour.
